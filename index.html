Synopsis

Cyber Sierra is an AI-powered cybersecurity platform built to make security compliance easy for enterprises. Given the nature of current attack vectors which are increasingly frequent, cyber compliance initiatives need to be continuous (vs point-in-time).

As enterprise teams increasingly require automation in controls monitoring - Cyber Sierra's capabilities, driven by AI, is welcomed by risk and information security professionals in global enterprises which include Fortune 500 companies and large SMBs.

Explore Universal Control Frameworks and leverage the best technologies to drive greater precision, accuracy in security controls at Cyber Sierra.

About the Program

Cyber Sierra is excited to announce the launch of an innovative program to engage top computer science students in solving cutting-edge adaptive cybersecurity problems at the intersection of AI.

Starting in Fall 2024, this initiative will provide students with real-world experience through complex and groundbreaking projects designed by Cyber Sierra. By investing in this program, you will be supporting the next generation of cybersecurity experts while contributing to the development of innovative solutions that address the ever-evolving challenges in the field.

Join us in this exciting venture and be a part of shaping the future of cybersecurity.
